Student life

I have been here my whole life

The campus of Red Lion Christian Academy is always teaming with life. Not only do we have an active campus during the school day, many opportunities are provided after school. Students are encouraged to have a well-rounded experience, explore their gifts and interests, and build memories. No matter what the context, teachers and staff provide a nurturing and caring environment for students to grow in the wisdom and knowledge of Jesus Christ. It is a blessing to see students overflow their love of Jesus into the lives of one another, their families, their communities, and abroad.

Red Lion Athletics
Sports

Red Lion Athletics exists to glorify God by training students to strive for athletic excellence while instilling character for continued service to Jesus Christ. A member of the Delaware Interscholastic Athletic Association (DIAA), Red Lion Athletics endeavors to excel in each sport at every level. Competing at the middle school, junior varsity, and varsity levels, teams are committed to developing skill, knowledge of the game, and competitive zeal.
